## Posture statement

The **Critical** residual risks (REM-1, REM-2) are all **sandbox-default** and are fully **mitigated by
deliberate configuration** (`sandbox.enabled=true`, `provider=docker`) plus the always-on approval gate
and audit log. With that configuration and single-instance operation, Nexus v1.0.1 carries **Medium/Low**
residual risk and is **STABLE** for single-operator governed execution. Out of the box, treat it as a
**host-executing, attended** console and restrict to trusted commands/repositories.
